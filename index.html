Carpentry and Joinery for Wheelchair Users

Blydens Carpentry and Joinery (BCJ) foundation aims to educate and train individuals with Spinal Cord Injuries (SCI) in the craft of carpentry, using materials and techniques to design, build, repair, maintain items associated with timber which require renovation, refurbishment, and restoration.
